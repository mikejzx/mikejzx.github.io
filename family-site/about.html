Who Are We?

We are the Smith family. There is little to talk about in regards to who we actually are, apart from the fact that we feel the need to have a family website - because of course, that's what families do, right?

Why Should I Care?

You shouldn't. There's nothing special about us. We're just an average family living in an average part of the world. We have nothing unique to offer to society.

Where We Came From

The Smith family originated some time in the 16th century, under brutal conditions in Siberia, Russia after John Smith engaged in an epic fight with a bear in which he had nearly lost his right arm. John's dog, Joe, is said to have scared off the bear, somehow, and saved John's life. John unexpectedly met his long-time partner, Jill, immediately after the battle. The two remained by each other's side from that day through to the end. (With their dog.) They started a family, the Smiths, and passed their story down through the generations.

Today, we are based in Victoria, Australia, and are living boring lives just like every other family in the country.
