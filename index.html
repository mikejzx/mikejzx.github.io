Tools++:
One of the larger projects of mine, this program shows a nice little RAM graph on your taskbar. The options should be pretty self-explanatory. If a setting is un-clear to you, just send me an e-mail about it and I'll try to explain it to you.
There are many colours to choose from, though due to the small size of the icon, it's best to choose highly-contrasting colours.
Note: The 'Show CPU load' option does not save when the program is closed, as it is experimental, and not intended to actually be used.

Download Tools++ v1.7.2
